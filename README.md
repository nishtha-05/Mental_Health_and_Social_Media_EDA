This project executes a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) with the help of Python, focused on the dynamic relationship between social media engagement and mental well-being.The core objective is to quantify the influence of various digital behaviors and lifestyle factors on mental health outcomes. This deep dive is driven by key metrics, including daily screen time, sleep quality, reported stress levels, and exercise frequency.The analysis aims to illuminate critical digital behavior patterns, uncover emerging mental health trends, and identify potential high-risk indicators associated with social media usage. Ultimately, the findings will establish a crucial foundation for developing actionable digital wellness strategies and predictive models for mental health assessment.
